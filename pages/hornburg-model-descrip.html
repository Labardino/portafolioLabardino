Hornburg Castle Project



This is a Maya 3D model of the Hornburg Castle from "The Lord of the Rings Tht Two Towers" movie. I decided I wanted to do a castle which would be of a higher difficulty. In the end I chose “The Hornburg Castle” from Lord of the Rings because of how much I loved and re-watched the battle scene from the movies.


Process description


My first main problem was that there weren’t many useful references. So after a day of scouring the internet, I decided to rewatch the scene. Once I saw the scene, I saw that, this was my best reference. It was as if I had the lego pieces (the movie shots) and I had to piece them together little by little as I would a Lego set. Nevertheless, I also did use a perspective shot from a Lord of the Rings videogame.
I spent many hours during this model, spending the most time building the front gate and the mountains. The mountains were not a part of the castle, but halfway through I figured that the Mountain may not be considered part of the castle but it was what it gave it its essence. Each time I felt it was done, I decided to do another part, and another wall etc. Always feeling I could do more.

Hardships

What I loved

What I learned

One challenging part of this project was how we could easily change the arrows directions without having to do it manually, while bug detection and error was also a difficult part.
I loved the fact of how we were building a whole universe from scratch and learning more aspects of Unity. However my favorite aspect was the programming and game-design.
This project showed me a glimpse of how much work there is to do in order to build a video game. I also learned how Unity works inlcuding sprites, prefabs, coding etc.
